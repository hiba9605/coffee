HOW TO MAKE

Step1:Grind the coffee
For a fresh and delicious coffee, you should be grinding your beans right before brewing.

Step2:Boil the water
Bring the water to a boil and let it cool for one minute, so it may reach a usable temperature.

Step3:Put coffee in water
It’s brew time! Take your ground coffee and scoop it into the middle of the boiled water.

Step4:Pour milk
It’s brew time! Take your fresh milk and pour it into the middle of the coffee cup.

Step5:Pour and wait
Go with the first pour, the so-called bloom pours resume pouring after 30 or 40 sec.
